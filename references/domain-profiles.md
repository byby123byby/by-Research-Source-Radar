# Domain Profiles

Choose one primary profile and add secondary profiles where the question crosses evidence cultures.

## Computing And Software

Search scholarly indexes, publisher libraries, preprints, GitHub/GitLab, package/model/dataset registries, benchmarks, organization pages, releases, issues, tests, licenses, and downstream dependencies.

Translation evidence may include architecture mapping, behavioral tests, failure tests, compatibility, migration, performance, security, accessibility, licensing, telemetry, and reproducibility.

Keep `direct_use` and `mechanism_transfer` separate. Do not discard a server/Python framework before appraising its algorithms or data structures.

## Health And Clinical Research

Search biomedical indexes, systematic-review databases, trial registries, guidelines, regulator notices, protocols, and adverse-event or retraction sources as appropriate.

Translation requires population/intervention/comparator/outcome fit, risk of bias, clinical relevance, safety, consent, governance, and professional review. Do not convert literature evidence directly into clinical advice or deployment claims.

## Human-Subjects, HCI, Psychology, And Social Science

Search discipline indexes, conference/journal libraries, preprints, instrument/scale sources, registered reports, datasets, and relevant grey literature.

Translation requires construct validity, population/context fit, recruitment, accessibility, ethics/consent, stopping rules, qualitative rigor, statistical power, and transparent separation of pilot engineering evidence from participant benefit.

## Experimental Science And Engineering

Search scholarly indexes, standards, apparatus documentation, datasets, protocols, calibration methods, and replication/failure reports.

Translation requires materials/apparatus equivalence, parameter ranges, calibration, controls, uncertainty, safety, reproducibility, and a traceable link from source conditions to local conditions.

## Education

Search education indexes, intervention studies, evidence syntheses, curricula, measurement instruments, policy/guideline sources, and implementation reports.

Translation requires learner/context fit, comparison condition, outcome validity, implementation fidelity, accessibility, ethics, and teacher/institution constraints.

## Law, Regulation, And Policy

Search primary law/regulation, official guidance, cases/decisions, government data, legislative history, standards, and peer-reviewed policy evidence. Record jurisdiction and effective date.

Translation requires authority hierarchy, jurisdiction, current validity, stakeholder impact, implementation feasibility, and explicit separation between legal information and professional legal advice.

## Business, Management, And Operations

Search peer-reviewed research, official/company filings, standards, reputable industry data, and methodologically transparent reports.

Translation requires source incentives, metric definitions, representativeness, causal limits, cost, implementation risk, and decision-specific uncertainty.

## Humanities, Languages, And Cultural Heritage

Search library catalogs, scholarly indexes, primary texts, critical editions, archival finding aids, digitized manuscripts, corpus repositories, citation networks, oral-history records, museum or collection metadata, and authoritative translations. Record the edition, language, provenance, archival status, access restrictions, and uncertainty in attribution or dating.

Translation requires historical and cultural context, source-language and translation limits, provenance, interpretive pluralism, positionality, copyright, and careful separation between an interpretation and an established fact. Do not infer causal or quantitative conclusions from interpretive evidence without an appropriate design.

## Arts, Design, And Media

Search museum and institutional records, exhibition catalogues, artist or designer archives, peer-reviewed visual-culture and design research, production credits, standards, repositories, portfolios, and documented practice-based research. Treat images, videos, generated outputs, and social posts as evidence only when their provenance, version, authorship, and context are recorded.

Translation requires object or media provenance, material and production constraints, cultural context, copyright and licensing, audience or user context, accessibility, and appropriate curatorial or disciplinary critique. A visually plausible generated artifact is not proof of historical authenticity, social impact, or design effectiveness.

## Multidisciplinary Work

Do not flatten standards of evidence. Maintain separate appraisal columns by domain, then synthesize. For example, a technically functioning health app still needs clinical, human-subject, privacy, and deployment evidence.
